# 🧠 Brainova — Digital Wellness & Focus App

**Brainova** is a premium Flutter application built to help users take back control of their digital habits, improve mental clarity, and build a healthier relationship with content consumption.

It combines **mind reset techniques**, **habit tracking**, and **gamification** into one seamless experience focused on cognitive well-being and digital detox.

---

## ✨ Overview

Brainova is not just a productivity app — it's a **cognitive wellness system** designed to:

* Reduce digital overload
* Improve focus and mental clarity
* Encourage mindful content consumption
* Build sustainable habits through motivation and rewards

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🧘 Mind Reset & Activities

* Guided audio & visual sessions (rain, nature, etc.)
* Smooth and immersive activity player
* Tools for reducing stress and mental clutter
* Techniques for improving long-term focus

---

### 🥗 Content Diet Tracking

* Track daily digital consumption
* Monitor online behavior patterns
* Visual insights and usage analytics
* Stay aware of your "content diet"

### 🏆 Gamification System

* Daily streak tracking

* Motivation through rewards and consistency

---

## 🧱 Tech Stack

* **Framework:** Flutter (SDK >= 3.2.0)
* **State Management:** Riverpod
* **Navigation:** GoRouter
* **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Storage, App Check)
* **Animations:** Rive & Lottie
* **Icons:** Lucide & Cupertino Icons
